Hi, I'm Mariam Tarhini

I am a certified NLP trainer and personal development coach, accredited as a Trainer and Master Practitioner by the International NLP Trainers Association. I also hold certifications in Emotional Intelligence, mBIT coaching, and John Maxwell's Person of Influence program.

I help individuals understand their inner patterns, build emotional strength, and create meaningful change in their personal and professional lives.

My approach is practical, supportive, and focused on empowering people to gain clarity, confidence, and stronger communication skills.

I speak Arabic, English, and French, and my professional background adds structure and depth to my coaching. My goal is to guide you toward becoming the most aligned and confident version of yourself. I'm here to empower you to unlock your full potential and achieve the life you deserve.

My Journey

As a devoted spouse and parent, my life's path has been profoundly shaped by my journey of personal growth and the experiences that forged my resilience. Over the years, I've transformed challenges into opportunities, learning valuable lessons that now resonate with those I serve.

Inspired by personal growth and the importance of mental resilience, I discovered the profound impact of coaching. I believe change starts from within, and by uncovering your strengths and addressing your challenges, we can create lasting transformation together.

My Guiding Principles

I am deeply committed to the belief that each person's journey is within their own hands. Every individual possesses the power to create the life they envision, and challenges are there to be overcome by selecting their dreams and intentions. As your coach, I am here to support you in your journey.

Your mind shapes your reality. Together, we'll uncover limiting beliefs, align your thoughts with your goals, and create a strategy that helps you move forward with confidence and clarity.
